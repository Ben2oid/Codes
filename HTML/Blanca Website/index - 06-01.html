About
me

I was born in 1998 in Vojvodina, Serbia. Since my early childhood years, I have been fascinated by the visual arts. At the age of 15, I started focusing on painting, and thanks to a great mentor, it became my passion and, by now, an irreplaceable part of my everyday life. I participated in several group exhibitions and had my first solo exhibition in 2016, in my hometown, Subotica.

My technique of choice is oil on canvas. I have been focusing on figurative painting, mainly portraits, as I am deeply interested in the effects of color, light, and emotions. What drives me is the process of experimentation, the never-ending road of exploring the human perception.
